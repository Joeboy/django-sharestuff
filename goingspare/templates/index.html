What is this thing?

ShareStuff is a Freecycle-inspired tool to help you share your unwanted stuff with your community. It's not yet finished, but it is already functional and useful. Please register, use it and send me any feedback.

This website allows you to:

  • Post descriptions and pictures of your spare stuff to the web;
  • Send details of your offers to email lists like Freecycle, Freegle etc;
  • See other people's offers.
