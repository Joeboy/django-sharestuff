What is this thing?

ShareStuff is a Freecycle-inspired tool to help you share your unwanted stuff with your community. At the moment it's still in development. It looks kind of horrible and has some rough edges, which I hope to fix at some point soon. It's already usable and useful though, so feel free to register, use it and send me any feedback.

This website allows you to:

  • Post descriptions and pictures of your spare stuff to the web;
  • Send details of your offers to email lists like Freecycle, Freegle etc;
  • See other people's offers.

I have various plans for making it do other cool things, but right now I have a house full of tat to get rid of so I'm restricting it to the bare necessities for now.

The source code for the site is freely available here. Feel free to do what you want with it.
